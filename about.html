Professional
I have been working in design for several years in a variety of industries. I started by designing playground layouts and components, retail store products, and the envirowork waste management systems. I eventually moved onto a design manager position at a company that manufactured wire mesh, screen and bar grating. I was responsible for implementing a new design system. This required the creation of design processes and libraries. In 2018 I had an opportunity to become a CAD Manager before I was quickly promoted to the Product Design Manager at a cannabis company. It was during my time here I was initially introduced to UX/UI design. I noticed many similarities in the design process from identifying the problem, researching, ideation, prototyping, testing, and handing off the design when everything is polished.

I am currently interested in full-time or freelance work. If you are interested in working with me, please contact me.
