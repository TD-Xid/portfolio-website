Project Introduction

Context

Fitness apps have become more and more popular over the years. Many of which have exercises and lessons a person can do directly from their phone. People have become empowered with the opportunity to work out from home using these apps, even take the exercises with them to the gym. The one thing they are lacking is a personal touch, with many routines focused on general fitness.

Why Spotter?

Spotter is a tool designed to help personal trainers connect with their client base in a new way. It acts as a bridge to keep clients accountable by providing the attention they require to achieve their goals. The trainers can create custom exercise videos and programs tailor-fit to each client's unique needs. All while enabling progress tracking through the program and multiple forms of communication.

Moodboard 01

This board has a dark theme, the images showcase a serious fitness feel and the text used specifically for the title has a very military or boot camp feel. With the darker theme, the idea of using neon effects would make elements pop.

Moodboard 02

This board embodies more of a lighthearted and inclusive theme. It shows supportive trainers encouraging their clients. Due to the above reasons, this theme was selected for the inspiration for this project.
